About EventFlow

EventFlow is a dynamic event scheduling platform designed to help users manage their events efficiently. Whether you're planning personal activities, work meetings, or social events, EventFlow keeps you organized.

Key Features

  • Add and manage events seamlessly.
  • Set reminders and never miss an important date.
  • Secure biometric authentication for easy access.
  • Edit and delete events with a simple interface.
  • AI-powered chatbot assistance for scheduling help.
